## What Makes This Different

Most compliance tools are static checklists. This one is interactive.

Toggle any of the 18 controls (8 POPIA + 10 GDPR) and watch the animated score rings update in real time. Built around the 2024 WhatsApp enforcement action — the first major tech enforcement under POPIA — and the key insight it revealed: a company can be GDPR-compliant in Europe and still critically violate POPIA in South Africa.

---

## The Case Study — WhatsApp Enforcement 2024

South Africa's Information Regulator found that WhatsApp applied full GDPR protections to European users but gave South African users a weaker standard under POPIA — despite both laws requiring the same level of protection.

- **2021** — Information Regulator begins engaging WhatsApp
- **April 2024** — Formal compliance assessment launched
- **10 September 2024** — Enforcement notice issued. 7 POPIA violations identified.
- **10 November 2024** — 60-day deadline to comply
- **April 2025** — Enforcement notice published publicly

**WhatsApp scored through this tracker:**

| Framework | Score | Rating |
|---|---|---|
| POPIA | 25% (2/8 passed) | Non-Compliant — Critical Risk |
| GDPR | 30% (3/10 passed) | Non-Compliant — Critical Risk |
| Overall | 27.5% | Non-Compliant — Critical Risk |

## POPIA Violations — WhatsApp 2024

| Section | Requirement | What WhatsApp Did Wrong |
|---|---|---|
| S.8 | Lawful basis | No lawful basis demonstrated |
| S.9 | Data minimisation | Collected more data than necessary |
| S.11 | Consent | Consent was forced and not informed |
| S.13 | Purpose specification | Did not explain why data was collected |
| S.15 | Further processing | Used data for new purposes without permission |
| S.17 | Openness | No records of processing activities |
| S.19 | Security safeguards | Weak technical and organisational measures |

## POPIA vs GDPR — Key Differences

| Element | POPIA (SA) | GDPR (EU) |
|---|---|---|
| Regulator | Information Regulator SA | National DPAs |
| Max fine | R10 million or 10% | €20M or 4% global turnover |
| Criminal liability | Up to 10 years imprisonment | Member state laws vary |
| Data officer | Information Officer (all orgs) | DPO (high-risk processing) |
| Breach notification | ASAP | 72-hour window |

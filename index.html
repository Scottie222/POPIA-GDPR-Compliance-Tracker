KEY LESSON — THE POPIA / GDPR GAP
GDPR (EUROPE) — COMPLIANT
WhatsApp applied full GDPR protections to European users. Transparent consent mechanisms. Clear data processing notices. Right to erasure implemented. Data minimisation enforced.
POPIA (SOUTH AFRICA) — NON-COMPLIANT
The exact same protections were NOT applied to SA users. Weaker consent. No PAIA manual. No Information Officer. No Personal Information Impact Assessment. POPIA requires the same standard as GDPR — WhatsApp assumed it didn't.
The gap this tracker detects: An organisation can score 80%+ on GDPR and still be critically non-compliant under POPIA. Local compliance is not automatic. This tracker scores both frameworks independently and highlights exactly where the gaps are.

PENALTY FRAMEWORK — POPIA ENFORCEMENT
ADMINISTRATIVE FINES
R10M
Maximum administrative fine per violation. Can be issued by the Information Regulator without criminal prosecution. Applies to organisations of any size operating in SA.
CRIMINAL LIABILITY
10 YRS
Maximum imprisonment for egregious POPIA violations. Applies to responsible parties and Information Officers personally. Significantly stronger than most data protection regimes globally.
COMPLIANCE ORDERS
60 DAYS
Deadline to submit compliance report after enforcement notice. Failure to comply escalates to criminal prosecution. WhatsApp was given 60 days from the September 2024 enforcement notice.
